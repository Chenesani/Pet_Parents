Who We Are

At Pet Parents, we believe pets are family. Since 2024, we’ve made it our mission to provide a curated collection of quality pet essentials that combine function, comfort, and personal style.

Headquartered in Gaborone, Botswana, our shop was founded out of a deep love for animals and a passion for beautifully crafted pet products. Whether you're shopping online or visiting our store, we strive to make your experience seamless and enjoyable.

We specialize in products made by independent designers — collars, leashes, beds, bowls, toys, and more — all chosen for their thoughtful design, durability, and charm.

Beyond retail, we are committed to our community. Pet Parents proudly supports animal rescue organizations through adoption events, awareness campaigns, and fundraising efforts. Our goal is to create a better world for pets and the people who love them.

Thank you for letting us be part of your pet’s journey. We’re so glad you’re here.
